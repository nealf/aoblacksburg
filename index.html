Who we are

The American Outlaws have a mission “to support the United States National Team through a unified and dedicated group of supporters. We are loud, passionate, organized, and dedicated.” We know there are a lot of fans in Blacksburg, Christiansburg, and the rest of the NRV, and we hope to unite us all by forming our own chapter of the American Outlaws (AO).

What we do

Our primary activities are watch parties for all of the US Men’s and Women’s soccer matches, where we all get together and cheer on the teams! Our Home Bar is Bull and Bones Restaurant in Blacksburg, who have graciously offered to accommodate us with their big screen TVs and audio for all of the games.

How to join in the fun

Come out to Bull and Bones during a US Soccer game to see what we’re all about! If you like what you see, you should definitely become an official member (and help us become an official chapter)! Membership dues are $29 and include some great benefits such as an official supporters t-shirt, a US flag bandana, discounts on everything from flights to rental cars, and much more! Our local chapter will also receive $5 of your dues to fund our very own activities and swag.

Find events on our Facebook page or follow us on Twitter to learn about upcoming games!
